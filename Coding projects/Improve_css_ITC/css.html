What are Skins in League of Legends?

A champion skin refers to an alternate appearance (skin) and/or color scheme (chroma) for any given League of Legends champion. Most skins can be purchased from the game client's Riot Store using RP (Riot Points), while some are only available for a limited time. A select few are unobtainable aside from the time they were first released.

Key Features of Skins:

- Each skin comes with its own unique model and splash art.

- Some skins offer different particle effects, voice-overs, and sound effects (price may vary).

- Chroma are a separate color palette for an existing skin or model.

Chroma are purchased in the same fashion as skins, and each costs 290 RP. This was introduced with the Chroma 2.0 update and can be purchased through bundles at a reduced price when bundled with the corresponding champion or skin.

Essence Emporium: Twice a year, an Essence Emporium event allows players to purchase a selection of cosmetics with Blue Essence (BE), including exclusive items and items that are normally only available with RP. The first Essence Emporium was held at the start of Season Eight, replacing the bi-annual Chroma IP Sales.

A chroma can be selected during champion select by clicking on the corresponding color tile on the champion/skin thumbnail.

Some skins are released as variants of other skins, for example:

- Worlds 2012 Riven and Urfwick, created to re-release previously limited edition skins while preserving the prestige of the original skins.

- Lancer Paragon Blitzcrank and Lancer Rogue Blitzcrank were the first instance of two skins released concurrently with a 30% discount if you already owned the other skin. Based on feedback, Riot has increased the discount to 45% for future variant skins (except for special releases).

All current and old splash artwork can be viewed on each champion's respective Skins subpage.

Trancendant

This tier transcends and overcomes the bounds of the game and is reserved for real-life events. Skins in this tier include all Ultimate skin features but also unique flavor for multiple different parts of gameplay. Outside of the game, releases are paired with an abundance of exclusive and non-exclusive content related to the event, whether it be for entertainment, collection, or commemoration, all meant to function as a landmark of the entire League of Legends community.
